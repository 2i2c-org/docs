# Get started with a new 2i2c hub service

These are steps to follow when you've just begun a new hub service as part of [membership with 2i2c](https://2i2c.org/membership).
If you're looking for a high-level overview first, see [2i2c's platform](https://2i2c.org/platform).

## What onboarding looks like

Onboarding is a collaborative process. After your membership is active, the 2i2c team will be in touch to coordinate next steps and gather the details needed to launch and operate your service.

For operational detail on how 2i2c carries out onboarding internally, see the Compass process: [Onboard a Community](https://compass.2i2c.org/services/interactive-computing/onboard/).

## Get support

Once the hub is up and running, you should use the 2i2c support channels to get support in modifying your hub.
See [](../support.md) for instructions on how to do this.
